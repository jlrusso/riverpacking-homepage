Why River Packing?

We offer something that many packing sheds can't, a way for growers and buyers to have access to the fruit that is being packaged and shipped that day. For example, we store and analyze data from the fruit such as size, color, damage or various types, flavor profile, among others. This enables you to see the quality of your fruit.

Products

Cherries

We start the summer by packing and shipping some of the best cherries from Sourthern California to the San Joaquin valley. Thanks to software provided by Unitec, we are able to maximize the concentration of high quality fruit. Besides the array of nutritional benefits, common varieties include Brooks, Royal Hazel, Tulare, Coral, Chelan, Bing, Laping, and Rainier.

Grapes

Although they were introduced to America almost 300 years ago, grapes are one of the most sold fruit in the states. We specialize in packing the very best grapes that come to our center so we can guarantee the satisfaction of our customers. Among thousands of grape varieties, we focus on Cinsaut, Gamay, Mencia, Mandilaria, Bobal, Ciliegiolo, Scheurebe, and Aramon.

Pears

From July to August, Pears are the main focus in our shed. Considered a sacred fruit in Greek and Roman mythology, we also value the Pear for its resilient characteristics. Around half of all pears sold in the United States are packaged and shipped by River Packing. Our varieties include Bartletts, Comice, Sunsprite, Seckel, French Butter, Anjou, Starkrimson, and Asian.
